What is Pi Coin?
Pi Coin is a digital currency developed by the Pi Network, aiming to make cryptocurrency accessible to the general public. It’s mined through the Pi Network app on mobile devices without draining much battery or requiring powerful hardware.
Can I withdraw or transfer my Pi Coins?
Yes, you can withdraw your Pi coins from your wallet and transfer them to other wallets once you've completed the required verification process.
When will Pi Coin be listed on exchanges?
There is no official timeline for when Pi Coin will be listed on exchanges. The team plans to complete several development phases, including moving to a mainnet, before any listings.
How do I start mining Pi?
To mine Pi, download the Pi Network app, create an account, and start mining with a simple tap every 24 hours. You’ll need to keep mining active daily, but the app runs in the background without heavy resource usage.
